Overview

Purpose

This website aims to introduce new fans to college football by explaining key concepts, rules, and traditions in an engaging and interactive way.

Audience

Newcomers to college football such as new students of a college with a football team, or international students unfamiliar with the sport.

Dynamic elements

The page will have many interactive parts such as a fun fact generator, and a quiz.

Content

Home page

The home page will include a header and intro message followed by a list of information like traditions, rules and differences from nfl. The fun fact randomizer will also be on this page. There will be images of football stadiums, marching bands, etc also on the page.

[Page 2]

This will be the rivalry subpage that will focus on talking about the history and impact of rivalry football games. There will also be a quiz on this page. Pictures will be of different college football rivalry team logos.
